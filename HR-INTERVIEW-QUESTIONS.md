## Suggested Questions to Ask HR Department

### 1. **Workflow & Process Questions**

#### Q1: Approval Workflow
"Can you walk us through the current travel request approval process? Who are the key approvers and in what order?"

**Why ask:** To ensure our system matches the actual workflow.

#### Q2: Budget Management
"How is the travel budget allocated and tracked? Is it per department, per semester, or per fiscal year?"

**Why ask:** To understand budget constraints and display requirements.

#### Q3: Emergency Requests
"How are urgent or emergency travel requests handled? Is there a different process or priority system?"

**Why ask:** To see if we need a "priority" or "urgent" flag in the system.

---

### 2. **User Roles & Permissions**

#### Q4: Role Assignment
"Who has the authority to approve travel requests at each level? Are there specific titles or positions that automatically have approval authority?"

**Why ask:** To correctly map roles (Head, Admin, Comptroller, HR, VP, President) in the system.

#### Q5: Delegation
"What happens when a department head or approver is on leave? Can someone else approve on their behalf?"

**Why ask:** To see if we need a "delegate" or "acting" approver feature.

#### Q6: Multi-Role Users
"Can one person have multiple roles? For example, can someone be both a Department Head and a Faculty member?"

**Why ask:** To handle dual-role scenarios correctly.

---

### 3. **Documentation & Requirements**

#### Q7: Required Documents
"What documents are required for travel requests? Are there different requirements for local vs. international travel?"

**Why ask:** To ensure we capture all necessary information in the form.

#### Q8: Signature Requirements
"Are physical signatures required, or are digital signatures acceptable? What about for approvals?"

**Why ask:** To confirm our digital signature implementation is acceptable.

#### Q9: Reporting
"What reports or analytics does HR need from the travel request system? Monthly summaries? Budget tracking?"

**Why ask:** To plan reporting features.

---

### 4. **Budget & Financial**

#### Q10: Budget Limits
"Are there spending limits per request or per person? What happens if a request exceeds the budget?"

**Why ask:** To implement budget validation and warnings.

#### Q11: Reimbursement Process
"How does the reimbursement process work? Is it integrated with the travel request, or is it separate?"

**Why ask:** To see if we need to add reimbursement tracking.

#### Q12: Budget Allocation
"How is the budget allocated to departments? Is it equal, or based on department size/needs?"

**Why ask:** To understand budget distribution logic.

---

### 5. **Notifications & Communication**

#### Q13: Notification Preferences
"How should approvers be notified of pending requests? Email? SMS? In-app notifications?"

**Why ask:** To implement the right notification channels.

#### Q14: Escalation
"What happens if a request is pending for too long? Is there an escalation process?"

**Why ask:** To add auto-escalation or reminder features.

#### Q15: Status Updates
"Should requesters be notified at each approval stage, or only when the request is fully approved/rejected?"

**Why ask:** To configure notification triggers correctly.

---

### 6. **Special Cases & Edge Cases**

#### Q16: International Travel
"Are there additional requirements or approvals needed for international travel? Different budget rules?"

**Why ask:** To handle international vs. local travel differently.

#### Q17: Group Travel
"How are group travel requests handled? One request per person, or one request for the group?"

**Why ask:** To see if we need group request features (like our seminar participant system).

#### Q18: Vehicle Requests
"What's the process for requesting institutional vehicles? Is there a separate approval for vehicle allocation?"

**Why ask:** To ensure vehicle request workflow is correct.

#### Q19: Returned Requests
"What happens when a request is returned to the requester for revision? Does it go back to the beginning of the workflow?"

**Why ask:** To confirm our "return to requester" logic is correct.

---

### 7. **Integration & Data**

#### Q20: Existing Systems
"Does HR use any existing systems for travel management? Do we need to integrate with payroll, accounting, or other systems?"

**Why ask:** To plan integrations if needed.

#### Q21: User Data Source
"Where does user information (name, department, position) come from? Is it from Azure AD, a separate HR system, or manually maintained?"

**Why ask:** To confirm our Azure AD integration approach.

#### Q22: Historical Data
"Do you have historical travel request data that needs to be migrated to the new system?"

**Why ask:** To plan data migration if needed.

---

### 8. **Compliance & Policies**

#### Q23: Policy Documents
"Are there official travel policies or guidelines documents we should reference? Can we get a copy?"

**Why ask:** To ensure the system enforces correct policies.

#### Q24: Audit Requirements
"Are there audit or compliance requirements for travel requests? Do we need to maintain specific records or logs?"

**Why ask:** To implement proper audit trails and logging.

#### Q25: Data Retention
"How long should travel request records be kept? Are there legal or policy requirements?"

**Why ask:** To plan data retention and archival.

---

### 9. **Training & Adoption**

#### Q26: User Training
"Will HR provide training for users on the new system? Or should we create training materials?"

**Why ask:** To plan documentation and training resources.

#### Q27: Rollout Plan
"How should we roll out the system? All at once, or phased by department? Any pilot groups?"

**Why ask:** To plan deployment strategy.

#### Q28: Support
"Who will provide user support after launch? IT? HR? Or should we include a help desk feature?"

**Why ask:** To plan support and help features.

---

### 10. **Future Enhancements**

#### Q29: Future Features
"Are there any features or improvements you'd like to see in the future? Mobile app? Advanced reporting? Integration with other systems?"

**Why ask:** To understand long-term vision and priorities.

#### Q30: Feedback Mechanism
"How should users provide feedback or report issues with the system?"

**Why ask:** To implement feedback and bug reporting features.

---

## Quick Checklist for Interview

**Before Interview:**
- [ ] Review current system features
- [ ] Prepare demo or screenshots
- [ ] List specific questions based on your concerns
- [ ] Bring notebook or device to take notes

**During Interview:**
- [ ] Ask open-ended questions
- [ ] Listen actively and take notes
- [ ] Ask follow-up questions if something is unclear
- [ ] Request documentation or policy copies if mentioned

**After Interview:**
- [ ] Review notes and identify action items
- [ ] Update system based on clarifications
- [ ] Follow up with HR if more questions arise

---

## Most Important Questions (Priority)

If time is limited, focus on these:

1. **Approval workflow** - Who approves what and in what order?
2. **Budget management** - How is budget allocated and tracked?
3. **Role assignment** - Who has approval authority at each level?
4. **Special cases** - International travel, group travel, emergencies
5. **Integration needs** - Do we need to connect with other systems?

---

## Tips for the Interview

1. **Be specific** - Ask about actual scenarios, not abstract concepts
2. **Show examples** - "In our system, we have this feature... is this correct?"
3. **Ask for clarification** - "Can you clarify what you mean by...?"
4. **Take notes** - Write down important details
5. **Follow up** - "If I have more questions later, can I contact you?"
