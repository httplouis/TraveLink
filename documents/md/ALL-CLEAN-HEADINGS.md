## Alternative: Find & Replace in Word

1. Press `Ctrl + H`
2. Click "More >>" button
3. Check "Use wildcards"
4. Find what: `7\.([0-9])\.%3`
5. Replace with: `7.\1`
6. Click "Replace All"

This will remove "%3" from all headings like "7.3.%3", "7.4.%3", etc.
